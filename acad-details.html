I just completed my second year at MIT.

I'm double majoring in Course 6-14 (Computer Science, Data Science, and Economics) and Course 18 (Mathematics), with a minor in Course 21M-1 (Music).

This summer, I am working through UROP (the Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program at MIT) to conduct research. I'm assisting Prof. David Autor of the Economics Department as well as PhD student Juliette Fournier in using geographical distributions and transportation systems to study labor markets in France. In addition, I'm working with the MIT Quest for Intelligence to develop model visualizations surrounding the current COVID-19 pandemic.
