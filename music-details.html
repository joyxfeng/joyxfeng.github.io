I'm active in the music community.

I'm classically trained in both piano (studying with John McCarthy at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music) and cello. I have previously played in the Lynbrook High School Chamber and Symphony Orchestras, performing in Barcelona, Zaragoza, and Valencia in Spain (2018, Principal Cellist) as well as Carnegie Hall in New York City (2016, Assistant Principal Cellist). In addition, I also played in the MIT Symphony Orchestra in their 2018-2019 season.

I have always loved chamber music -- I'm currently playing in the MIT Chamber Music Society as part of a piano trio, working on Dvorak's Piano Trio No. 3 in f minor. In addition, I'm one of the founding exec members and the ensemble director of MIT Cello++, MIT's one-and-only cello ensemble.

I love to arrange music with my friends in my free time, translating pop, soundtrack, and video game music to classical instruments and techniques.
