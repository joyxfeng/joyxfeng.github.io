About Me


Hello and welcome to my website! I'm Joy, a student in the MIT class of 2022. I grew up in the warmth of San Jose, California, but am now currently freezing in the winter of Cambridge, Massachusetts.

I like studying math, economics, computer science, music, French, and art history and a lot of other things. I also love traveling, dance, calligraphy, cooking, and classical music. If you want to check out my life as a student (pictures of my notes, little studying tips, etc.), please click this link to my studyblr!

I'm a choreographer for MIT's "Asian Dance Team", as well as a member of the Boston-based pre-professional dance group, "Dance Revelasian". In addition, I am a cellist in the MIT Symphony orchestra, and a member of the Undergraduate Association's Innovation and Marketing committees.
